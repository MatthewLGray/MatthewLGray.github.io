A Deep Dive into Syracuse's Winter Water Main Break Problem

For the past two decades, Syracuse and its surrounding residential areas have struggled against consistent winter water main breaks. Through my research and discussion with sources regarding the topic, I discovered that from 2004-2019, Syracuse has seen 82% more water main breaks in the winter months versus the summer months.
By Matthew Gray
